Rails World

Shaping the future of Ruby on Rails.

What is Rails World?

Rails World is an annual two-day, two track community conference featuring technical talks, demos, workshops, networking, and keynotes about the latest features and best practices in Rails development.

Next up.

Rails World 2026 will take place in Austin, TX in September 2026. Keep an eye on the website or sign up to the event mailing list for updates.
