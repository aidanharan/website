Rails at Scale

A gathering for engineers solving big challenges with Ruby on Rails.

Rails at Scale was a day of open discussion about scaling Rails applications. Itâ€™s where engineers working on high-performance, high-traffic systems connected and exchanged insights, best practices, and hard-earned lessons.

When

The Rails at Scale Summit took place the day before Rails World on Sept 3. It was an all day event, followed by a happy hour for attendees.

Thirty six companies gathered to:

  • Learn from other engineers who have scaled Rails in production
  • Share their own insights in open discussions
  • Connect with engineers solving similar challenges at other companies
  • Chat with the Rails Core, Committers and Issues teams

Due to space, this was an invite-only event capped at 100 attendees.

Rails at Scale was sponsored and facilitated by Shopify.
